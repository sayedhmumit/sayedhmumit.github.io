Currently pursuing a B.Sc. (Hons.) in Biochemistry and Biotechnology at the University of Science and Technology Chittagong. His academic focus covers RNA biology, genomics, CRISPR, immunology, and phytochemistry, supported by active research under the Bangladesh Bioscience Research Group (BBRG), where he serves as a Research Assistant and Scientific Grants Management Officer. Also, He is now an Academic Panelist at The Cambridge Panel Academic organized by Cambridge University Press.

His early recognition came through success in various national and international Olympiads, including being a medalist in the 16th National Bangladesh Astro-Olympiad (2022). These achievements earned him notable visibility in Bangladesh’s student and science communities, where he became known for his leadership, initiative, and consistent excellence in ECA (Extracurricular Activities).

As a peer reviewer for international journals and the author of two educational books—“From Concept to Publication” and “Meta Analysis of Skills”—Huzaifa has also made his mark as an educator and mentor. His entrepreneurial co-founding both A TimesM Global, and Manoshota, a mental health initiative that reached thousands worldwide.

Recognized with prestigious scholarships and fellowships from the American Physical Society, Wikipedia, and the Oxbridge Scholars Program, Huzaifa continues to balance academics, leadership, and research. His involvement in international programs such as the Wiki Scientist Summer Program further reflects his growing global engagement.
